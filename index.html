Even Temperature Zone

Objective

To find the even temperature zone of a tubular furnace.

Apparatus used:

Tubular furnace, Voltmeter, Thermostat, Thermocouple, Stand.

Description The temperature in a tubular furnace varies from one end to the other due to various heat losses. The losses include radiation and convection. Hence it becomes essential to find a zone in a furnace where the temperature practically remains constant.
